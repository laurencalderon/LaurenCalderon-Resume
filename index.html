Lauren Calderon

Media and Culture Studies

My name is Lauren Angela Calderon. I was born in Charlotte, North Carolina, but moved to Los Angeles, California four years later. Growing up, I have always been interesed in art. Whether it be through school projects or piano recitals, whenever I had the chance to showcase my creative abilities, I took full advantage of it.

As I got older, I was able to use my graphic designing skills in instances when clubs at my high school needed a new logo or when my Girl Scout troop needed to create a new design for a sweater.

One of my strong suits is working on a time crunch. As an aspiring graphic designer, it is prominent to be able to work in time constraining conditions. By doing so, I make sure to set aside time for specific clients to discuss the design and any changes they may want to make. Being able to work with different people is one of the many things that I enjoy about graphic design. Collaborating on ideas in order to create something special is very meaningful and inspiring for me and my work.
